CESI

Presentation

CESI is a school specialized in engineering, managerial staff, technicians and agent-control. CESI has five type of brands: ei.CESI, exia.CESI, CESI Enterprises, CESI work-study, and CESI Certification. It is spread all over France, Spain, Algeria and Cameroon with 29 campus.

History

The idea of CESI is born at the end of the 50' with the will of 5 big french industrial companies to allow their technicians to become ingeneers.
Today, CESI is 24 000 apprentices, 56 diplomas, 1 000 salaried employees, 2 800 speakers, 73 000 graduates, 100 partner universities all over the world and 10 000 partner companies
