Website introduction

What is T-MOBILE

Created in 1991 T-MOBILE is a subsidiary of Deutsche Telekom. It is a network operator. Originally T-MOBILE was located in Bonn, Germany Today the company is present worldwide, the name changed in 2002 from Deutsche Telekom to T-MOBILE.

The website goal

The main goal of the website is to sell plan to the users. Their primary target are people looking for a new plan, unhappy with their current one or people looking for a plan because they just got to the US. The website also offers phones with different credit options, tablets, and many other accessories.

The design strategy

T-MOBILE website has 2 main colors: black and pink, those colors are present everywhere on the website. It is also using a lot of images to attract the users, using as less texts as possible so the users have no thinking to do. Also, the website is very transparent about their prices and how it is less expensive than his competitors

Conclusion

T-MOBILE made a clear chose for their website, with a homepage full of commercial deals, a menu clear and with an easy path for phones and plans. You can chance easily the language and find the store with a few clicks only. With that in mind I would change the menu to be even more straighforward, reduce the size of the images on the homepage, make sure it's on the subject. The help is a bit too much intrusive, offer a phone number when necessary and maybe a way to open a chat, not forcefully open it.
