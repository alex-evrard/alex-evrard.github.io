Project idea

LocArt is a website that allows customers to get in touch with a large choice of artists. The main goal is to offer the artists some visibility that they don't have yet while allowing users to easily find artists.

One of the reason is the fact that some "young" artists may struggle at their beginning. The website is offering the possibility for them to get known.

The other reason is to allow people who want to organize a party to find many different type of artists and to find artists that may be more in their price range.

Conclusion

Use

Back office

  • The back office of the website is to handle both tables : artists and customers and the grades / comments
  • Two actions are possible : to delete or to update/modify
  • A javascript function add some protection to the tables with a pop up showing when a request is send, avoiding miss clicks.

Front

  • The search bar allow the users to find artists with two criterias : geographical and type of artist
  • Locart also organize showing of different artists, new ones, featured ones and some types of artist maybe not popular at the time, which will allow new upcoming artists to get some viewage and customers to explore and widen their choice
  • A grading and comment system is present to allow customers to express their satisfaction or dissatisfaction of the artists they hired. These grades and comments are attached to the artists and are watched by the admins to avoid abuse.
